Help Researching

Use this tool to create a dump of an original Anycubic spool tag to support the research for this app. The dump of one of the two spool sides is enough.

You can hand in your dumps by sending them via email to anycubic-nfc-research@molodos.com. Please include details on which exact spool you scanned (material, color, etc.).

Thanks for your support!
